Caprese Appetizer

Description

You can make a colorful and simple-to-serve Caprese Appetizer ahead of time and store it in a baking dish so it stays coated in olive oil to keep the basil green.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons fresh ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Do something else
  3. Place the olive oil in a baking dish
  4. Stir in the olive oil until golden brown
  5. Stir in the garlic powder
  6. Stir in the black pepper
  7. Stir in the pepper
  8. Eat and enjoy it!!!!
